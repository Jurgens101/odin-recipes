Rice!

Description:

Ingridients:

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • 50ml olive oil
  • 5g salt

Steps:

  1. Rinse the rice first with cold water on a strainer and let it dry
  2. Heat the olive oil at 120 degrees for 2 minutes on a pot
  3. Add the rice in the pot and slowly mix it untill it turns yellow-ish
  4. Add the water in the pan and wait for 10 minutes
  5. After 10 minutes remove pot from the eye for 5 minutes
  6. After 5 minutes put the pot on the warm eye and let the rice dry and ENJOY
