Omelette!

Description:

An omelet is a dish you might order for breakfast or

brunch â€” it's kind of like a folded pancake

made of beaten eggs, sometimes with the addition of

vegetables, cheese, or meat. Yum. A good omelet is a

little bit fluffy, but firm enough to contain its filling.

Ingridients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 300g mashrooms
  • 50ml olive oil
  • 3g salt
  • 1g pepper powder

Steps:

  1. First we slice the vegetables in small pieces
  2. Heat an pan with the olive oil for 2 minutes
  3. Add the mashrooms in the pan for 2 minutes
  4. Add the garlic and the onion
  5. Scramble the eggs in a small bowl
  6. Add the eggs and mix all the ingridients together for 1 minute
  7. Add the salt and pepper powder
  8. Flip the omelette and wait 1 minute
  9. Flip it again and wait 1 more minute
  10. Your omelette is ready. ENJOY!
