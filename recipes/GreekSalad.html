Greek Salad

Greek Salad!

Discription:

Greek salad or horiatiki salad is a popular salad in Greek cuisine generally

made with pieces of tomatoes, cucumbers, onion, feta cheese,

and olives and dressed with salt, Greek oregano, and olive oil.

Common additions include green bell pepper slices or caper berries

Go ahead and make one yourself. Its prety simple and fast.

Ingridients:

  • 1 Slice of Greek Feta
  • 3 Medium Tomatoes
  • 1 Cucumber
  • 1/2 Green Peper
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 100g Olives
  • 1 Teaspoon Oregon
  • 50ml Olive Oil
  • 3g Salt

Steps:

  1. Cut the tomatoes into small pieces
  2. Slice the onion, the peper and the cucumber
  3. Put them into a bowl
  4. Add the feta on top
  5. Add the olive oil and the oregon.
  6. Optionaly you can add the salt
  7. Mix them all together and ENJOY!
